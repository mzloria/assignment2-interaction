Game of Life: When animation is running, grid allows click capability to toggle a cell dead or alive for one frame.
Click "Start" to start animation, and click "Stop" to stop the animation. Click one of the patterns below
to restart Game of Life with the specified pattern. Grid lines can be toggled on/off after the animation has
started. Click the "Save" button to save the state of the board, and "Load" to load the state back onto the board.
